Same taste, faster delivery.

The student and teacher population of the “TEC de Monterrey” are tired of having to wait in line for their lunch to arrive at the different food establishments at the school. We are now offering an innovative way of improving the buyer experience.

Long queues to get your food? Are you late to class because you were waiting for your order? No more with the new SmartServe App, the app that will make your days in line fade into oblivion.

How it works.

Inside the Tec there are plenty of places to eat, all of which are always full. So we present the students and staff an alternative to ordering their food. They get connected via Wi-Fi with the different snacks and place your order, specify the time you pick it up, and your food is ready when you are. It will reduce your waiting drastically and you won’t be late for your classes anymore.
